Naming

This page describes the naming for the stuff in the emulator and this manual

Data types

Byte 8 bits
Word/Short 16 bits
Long 32 bits

About expressions

Addressing modes

Notation: an '*' means any number between 0 and 7
Notation: two '*' means any data or address register
Notation: an "<EXPR>" means any integer expression with label resolution


Data register Anything in the form of d*
Address register Anything in the form of a*
Address Anything in the form of (a*)
Address with Pre-decrement Anything in the form of -(a*)
Address with Post-increment Anything in the form of (a*)+
Address with Offset/Displacement Anything in the form of <EXPR>(a*)
Address with Index Anything in the form of (<EXPR>, a*, **) or (a*, **)
Absolute Short Any <EXPR> which can fit a Word
Absolute Long Any <EXPR> which can fit a Long
Immediate Any #<EXPR>
